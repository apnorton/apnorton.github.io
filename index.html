About Me

Hi! My name is Andrew Norton, and I am currently a second-year student at University of Virginia pursuing a BS in Computer Science. With a strong background in competitive robotics, I'm interested in machine learning and artificial intelligence.

As an active member of ACM@UVa, the school's chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, I have competed in the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC). My experience in competition programming was one of the primary reasons I first considered computer science. This past semester, the team consisting of myself and two others placed 4th of approximately 180 at the ICPC Mid-Atlantic Regional.
